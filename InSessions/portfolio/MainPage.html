General Information

Hi, I’m Adam, a computer science enthusiast with a passion for math and physics. I enjoy exploring complex problems, from Quarks in an atom to the intricacies of coding, constantly challenging myself to grow and learn. My journey in technology and science has been fueled by curiosity and the drive to solve real-world problems, and I aim to contribute meaningful solutions through my work. When I’m not diving into equations or coding, you’ll often find me reading about energy theories or playing chess. I believe in the power of knowledge and persistence, and I’m excited to connect with others who share my love for logical exploration.

Passion

I’ve always been passionate about math and physics, especially when it comes to concepts like logarithmic functions and their real-world applications. I find the process of proving equations, y= -x+2 being an asymptote to curve (C) incredibly rewarding—it’s like uncovering the limits of a function and understanding its behavior at infinity. The energy theorem also excites me, as it brings together abstract principles and practical use cases in physics. I’m particularly intrigued by how we calculate the magnetic field produced by a capacitor; it’s amazing how math and physics can explain the forces that govern our universe.

Soft-Skills
